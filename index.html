    • OVERVIEW

    Sunrise enterprise started its operation after its acquisition in 2013 to provide maintenance support to customers for engine generators and other electrical and mechanical appliances in airports and private industries with a goal to provide efficient service at a nominal price. Since then, it is a leading name in the power industry having customers comprising defense services, embassies, real estate, government, and private organizations.


    Eventually, we enlisted with CAAB as A–class contractor in 2014, and since then we have been participating in tenders in various Electical Mechanical(EM) works relevant to maintenance/repair/supply at different airports and so far we have completed awarded works on time successfully.

    On-call method - allows customers to call the technical team for support. The field support people provide service and maintenance when requires and are charged for the completion of that particular job.


    Contracted service - customers have to sign a formal agreement with Sunrise Enterprise for a certain period of time specifying terms and conditions of service and maintenance. Contracted service may include scheduled maintenance, unscheduled maintenance, health check, and troubleshooting. During the contracted period field support people will visit the customer’s site as per the agreed schedule and provide service and maintenance accordingly.

    Sunrise Enterprise and its workforce are running under leadership who has engineering, business administration, and management backgrounds with long working experience in a multinational company, a senior fellow of the institute of engineers, Bangladesh, well connected with power/it industries, businesses, and engineering bodies.
